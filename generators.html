How to generate a QR code

  1. Type or paste any text or URL into the input field.
  2. The QR code updates live as you type.
  3. Click "Download PNG" to save it to your device.

Frequently asked questions

Do QR codes expire?

No, these are static QR codes generated locally — they don't expire and aren't tracked by us.

Are generated passwords stored anywhere?

No. Passwords are generated using your browser's cryptographically secure random number generator and never leave your device.

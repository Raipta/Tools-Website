About Workbench

A small set of tools, built around one idea.

Workbench is a collection of free file tools — PDF, image, text and generators — built on one simple principle: your files are yours, and they should never have to leave your device just to be edited.

Why we built this

Most "online tool" websites quietly upload your files to a server to process them. We wanted something different — tools that run entirely inside your browser using modern web technology, so your documents and images stay private by default.

How it works

Every tool on this site uses client-side JavaScript libraries to read, transform and export your files directly on your device. No file is ever transmitted to our servers or any third party as part of using a tool.

Who it's for

Students, freelancers, small business owners, and anyone who needs a quick, reliable tool without installing software or signing up for an account.

  1. Paste or type your text into the Word Counter box.

  3. Estimated read time is calculated automatically based on average reading speed.

Frequently asked questions

Is my text stored or sent anywhere?

No. All text tools run entirely in your browser using JavaScript — nothing is transmitted or saved.

Does Text Compare check for plagiarism?

It highlights word-level differences between two texts you provide; it does not search the internet for matches.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Workbench ("we", "us") handles information when you use our website and tools.

Files you process with our tools

All PDF, image and text tools on this site run entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Files you upload to a tool (such as a PDF or image) are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers or any third party.

Information we may collect

We may use standard analytics tools to understand aggregate site usage, such as which pages are visited and approximate location/device type. This data does not include the content of any file you process with our tools.

Cookies

We may use cookies for basic site functionality (such as remembering your light/dark theme preference) and, where applicable, for displaying advertisements through third-party ad networks such as Google AdSense.

Third-party advertising

This site may display ads served by third-party vendors, including Google. These vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Data retention

Because file processing happens locally in your browser, we do not store, retain, or have access to the files you process using our tools.

Children's privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@workbench.example.com.
